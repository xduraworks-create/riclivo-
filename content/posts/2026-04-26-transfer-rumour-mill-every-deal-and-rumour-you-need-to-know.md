As we dive into another week of transfer speculation and confirmed moves, the football world remains abuzz with activity. Let’s break down the latest in the transfer market, highlighting confirmed deals, hot rumours, players to keep an eye on, and deals that fell through.

## Confirmed Deals

### Premier League
- **Jude Bellingham to Real Madrid**: The young English midfielder has officially completed his move to the Spanish giants for a reported fee of €100 million, alongside potential add-ons. This transfer marks a significant statement from Los Blancos, adding to their already star-studded lineup.
  
### La Liga
- **James Rodríguez to Al Rayyan**: James has officially made his move to Qatar, leaving behind European football for the moment. The Colombian playmaker joins Al Rayyan on a free transfer after his stint at Everton.

### Serie A
- **Paulo Dybala to AS Roma**: In a surprise move, Dybala has officially signed with AS Roma on a free transfer. The Argentine forward was released by Juventus and has already made an impact in the pre-season matches.

### Bundesliga
- **Sadio Mané to Bayern Munich**: The Senegalese forward has completed his switch from Liverpool to Bayern for a fee around €32 million. His addition is expected to boost Bayern’s attacking prowess significantly.

### Ligue 1
- **Neymar to Al-Hilal**: The Brazilian superstar has officially signed with Al-Hilal after multiple seasons at Paris Saint-Germain. The transfer fee is reported to be around €90 million, making it one of the most lucrative moves of the summer.

## Hot Rumours

### Premier League
- **Declan Rice to Arsenal**: (HOT) With the Gunners keen to bolster their midfield, whispers of a big-money move for Rice have intensified. West Ham could demand upwards of £80 million for their star midfielder, making this a potential record for the club.

- **Mo Salah to Al-Ittihad**: (WARM) Rumours are swirling about a possible departure for Salah, with Al-Ittihad reportedly preparing a lucrative offer. While Liverpool has publicly stated they do not plan to sell, the right offer could test their resolve.

### La Liga
- **Kylian Mbappé to Real Madrid**: (HOT) The dream move that fans have envisioned for years appears to be back on. Real Madrid is reportedly preparing a bid, potentially exceeding €200 million, despite PSG’s insistence on retaining their star forward.

- **João Félix to Barcelona**: (WARM) Barcelona is interested in bringing Félix to the Camp Nou, with discussions underway. However, financial fair play regulations could complicate this move.

### Serie A
- **Victor Osimhen to Manchester United**: (HOT) With United on the lookout for a new striker, Osimhen has emerged as a leading target. Napoli may demand around €100 million for the Nigerian forward, but United sees him as a potential key player for their future.

- **Milan Skriniar to PSG**: (COLD) While there were strong links between PSG and Skriniar, the Nerazzurri’s reluctance to sell has dampened the momentum. The move seems unlikely now, but stranger things have happened.

### Bundesliga
- **Marcus Thuram to Bayern Munich**: (WARM) Thuram has been linked with a move to Bayern, especially after they failed to secure a few other targets. A fee around €30 million could see the French international don the Bavarian colors next season.

### Ligue 1
- **Randal Kolo Muani to PSG**: (HOT) As PSG looks to revamp their squad, Kolo Muani has emerged as a key target. Eintracht Frankfurt may be willing to negotiate for around €70 million, which reflects his impressive performances last season.

## Ones to Watch

- **Gavi (Barcelona)**: With his performances on the field gaining attention, Gavi is increasingly being eyed by several top clubs across Europe. His contract situation at Barcelona is worth monitoring, as a potential move could reshape midfield dynamics at any club.

- **Yunus Musah (Valencia)**: After a strong World Cup performance, Musah has garnered interest from various clubs, including AC Milan and Arsenal. His versatility makes him a valuable asset moving forward.

- **Gabriel Jesus (Arsenal)**: Following an injury-stricken season last year, if he can find his form again, expect clubs to circle him as a potential target. His pace and finishing ability are attractive traits that many teams value.

## Deals That Fell Through

- **Dani Olmo to Barcelona**: The Spanish international was thought to be a target for Barcelona, but negotiations broke down due to financial constraints. While there was mutual interest, it seems Barcelona will need to focus elsewhere for the time being.

- **Alejandro Garnacho to Real Madrid**: Despite initial interest from Madrid, Manchester United’s firm stance on not selling their young talent has brought this potential deal to a standstill. Madrid will likely shift their focus to other targets now.

- **Youri Tielemans to Aston Villa**: The Belgian midfielder was set to make a move to Villa Park, but discussions collapsed over personal terms. With other teams now in the mix, it remains to be seen where Tielemans will end up.
