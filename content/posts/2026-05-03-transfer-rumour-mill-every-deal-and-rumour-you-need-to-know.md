As the winter transfer window closes, clubs are scrambling to finalize their rosters ahead of the second half of the season. Here’s a look at confirmed deals, the hottest rumours, players to keep an eye on, and those moves that didn’t quite make it across the line.

## Confirmed Deals

1. **Liverpool Signs Kone-Doherty**  
   Liverpool FC has officially signed winger Trent Kone-Doherty from their youth ranks to Molde FK. This move is seen as a strategic decision to allow the 18-year-old to gain first-team experience abroad.

2. **Norwich City’s Josh Sargent Joins Toronto FC**  
   American international Josh Sargent has completed his move to Toronto FC, marking a significant transfer that sees the forward moving to Major League Soccer (MLS) after a productive spell in the EFL Championship.

3. **Barcelona Add Tavares from Norwich**  
   FC Barcelona has confirmed the signing of young prospect Ajay Tavares from Norwich City. The 19-year-old is viewed as a future star and adds depth to Barcelona’s attacking options.

4. **Antonio’s Move to Al-Sailiya**  
   Jamaican forward Michail Antonio is set to join Qatari club Al-Sailiya after negotiations with Charlton Athletic fell through. The transfer represents a new chapter in Antonio's career as he looks to take on a new challenge in the Middle East.

5. **Sheffield Wednesday Signs Silcott-Duberry**  
   Sheffield Wednesday has brought in forward Zain Silcott-Duberry from Bournemouth, aiming to bolster their attacking line for the remainder of the season.

6. **El Khannouss Makes Stuttgart Loan Permanent**  
   Bilal El Khannouss has had his loan spell at VfB Stuttgart made permanent after an impressive run of form in the Bundesliga.

7. **Griezmann Heads to MLS**  
   Antoine Griezmann's highly anticipated switch from Atlético Madrid to Orlando City has been finalized. The French international is expected to make an immediate impact in the MLS.

## Hot Rumours

1. **Barcelona Eyeing Cristian Romero (HOT)**  
   Reports surface that Barcelona is keen on signing Tottenham’s central defender Cristian Romero this summer. The Spanish side sees Romero as a critical addition to solidify their backline, especially with key players aging.

2. **Liverpool Pursuing Francisco Conceição (WARM)**  
   Liverpool is reportedly interested in Porto's winger Francisco Conceição. The Reds view him as a long-term replacement for the departing Sadio Mané. While no formal offer has been made, discussions are said to be underway.

3. **Manchester United Considering a Move for Victor Osimhen (COLD)**  
   Rumours have circulated that Manchester United is monitoring Napoli forward Victor Osimhen. However, given Napoli’s reluctance to sell and United’s prior spending on attacking players, this deal appears unlikely at present.

4. **Juventus Looking at West Ham's Declan Rice (WARM)**  
   Juventus are reportedly preparing a substantial bid to lure Declan Rice to Serie A. Given Rice’s performances in the Premier League, this rumour carries weight, especially with Juventus looking to revitalize their squad.

5. **AC Milan Targeting Mikel Oyarzabal (WARM)**  
   AC Milan is said to be exploring a move for Real Sociedad's Mikel Oyarzabal. The Spanish international would be an exciting addition to Milan’s attack, and with his contract situation, he could be available at a reasonable price.

6. **Arsenal In Talks for Wilfried Zaha (WARM)**  
   Arsenal is reportedly back in discussions with Crystal Palace regarding a potential transfer for Wilfried Zaha. The winger's explosive style fits Mikel Arteta’s vision, but financial concerns could delay any concrete movement.

## Ones to Watch

1. **Jesse Lingard (Free Agent)**  
   After leaving Manchester United last summer, Lingard remains a free agent and is expected to attract interest from clubs throughout Europe, especially in the English lower leagues and overseas.

2. **João Pedro (Potential Move)**  
   Watford’s João Pedro has been linked with multiple clubs, including Barcelona. His impressive performances in the Championship have drawn attention, making his future worth following closely.

3. **Bilal El Khannouss (Permanent Signing Impact)**  
   With El Khannouss's recent permanent move to Stuttgart, it will be interesting to observe how he settles in and performs in the Bundesliga, potentially becoming a key player for the team.

4. **Victor Osimhen (Continued Interest)**  
   Despite the cold rumours, Osimhen's performances for Napoli continue to draw interest. His goal-scoring prowess makes him a sought-after talent, and his future remains a topic of interest.

5. **Josh Sargent in MLS**  
   After his move to Toronto FC, Sargent’s adaptation to a new league will be closely watched. His development could be crucial for both his career and potential future national team selections.

## Deals That Fell Through

1. **Lingard to Corinthians**  
   A potential switch to Corinthians failed to materialize, as both parties could not agree on terms. Lingard is still searching for a new club, making this a story to follow.

2. **Havertz’s Loan to Monaco**  
   A planned loan move for Chelsea's Kai Havertz to AS Monaco fell through at the last minute due to a disagreement over wages. This has left Havertz’s future at Chelsea uncertain as the summer approaches.

3. **Ronaldo Return to Juventus**  
   Speculations around Cristiano Ronaldo returning to Juventus have been quashed as both clubs reportedly could not agree on a feasible contract. This marks yet another chapter in the saga of one of football’s greatest.

4. **David De Gea’s Exit from Manchester United**  
   The proposed move for goalkeeper David De Gea to a foreign club fell through last week. De Gea remains at Old Trafford as Erik ten Hag looks to retain his experienced squad for the remainder of the season.

5. **Neymar’s Potential Return to Barcelona**  
   Speculations about Neymar returning to Barcelona were rife, but they have largely dissipated, as financial constraints and PSG's unwillingness to part with their star forward have scuttled the deal.

As we progress through the season, expect these narratives to evolve, with clubs making further strides in the transfer market. Stay tuned for more updates, as football never sleeps, and neither does the transfer mill.
