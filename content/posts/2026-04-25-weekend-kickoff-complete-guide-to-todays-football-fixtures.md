## Premier League Fixtures

### 1. Manchester City vs. Fulham  
**Kick-off:** 12:30 PM BST / 7:30 AM EST  
Manchester City, reigning champions, host a Fulham side that has struggled to find its footing this season. Expect City to assert their dominance early on.

**Key Players to Watch:**  
Erling Haaland has been in sensational form, already netting 10 goals this season. For Fulham, Aleksandar Mitrović will be key, looking to exploit any defensive lapses from City.

**Prediction:** Manchester City 3 - 1 Fulham. City’s attacking prowess should see them through comfortably, though Fulham could snatch a goal on the counter.

### 2. Chelsea vs. Brighton  
**Kick-off:** 3:00 PM BST / 10:00 AM EST  
Chelsea are on a quest for consistency, hosting an exciting Brighton side known for their tactical flexibility. This match has the makings of a classic.

**Key Players to Watch:**  
Raheem Sterling is crucial for Chelsea, capable of breaking defenses with his pace. On the other side, Brighton’s Kaoru Mitoma has been a revelation, bringing flair and creativity to the attack.

**Prediction:** Chelsea 2 - 2 Brighton. Both teams are evenly matched, making for an entertaining draw filled with chances.

### 3. Tottenham Hotspur vs. Liverpool  
**Kick-off:** 5:30 PM BST / 12:30 PM EST  
A massive clash at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium as Spurs take on Liverpool. Both teams are in the hunt for top-four spots, making this a must-watch fixture.

**Key Players to Watch:**  
Harry Kane is always a threat, and his partnership with Heung-min Son could trouble the Liverpool defense. For the visitors, Mohamed Salah is the key man, capable of turning a game with moments of brilliance.

**Prediction:** Tottenham 1 - 2 Liverpool. Liverpool’s experience and attacking depth may just edge them ahead in what promises to be a tightly contested match.
