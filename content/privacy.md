## Privacy Policy — Riclivo.online

*Last updated: April 12, 2026*

Riclivo.online is operated by **RicLivo Technologies**. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you visit our blog.

## Information We Collect

**Information you provide voluntarily:**
When you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a comment through our Google Form, we collect your name and email address. This information is stored securely in Google Sheets and used only to deliver the newsletter and respond to your comments.

**Information collected automatically:**
When you visit Riclivo.online, we may automatically collect standard web server logs including your IP address, browser type, pages visited and time spent on the site. This information is used solely to improve our content and user experience.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Send you our newsletter (only if you subscribed)
- Respond to your comments or questions
- Understand how our content performs
- Improve the blog experience for all readers

We **never** sell, trade or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Cookies

Riclivo.online may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies are used for analytics purposes (via Google Analytics) and to remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

- **Google Analytics** — for website traffic analysis
- **Google Forms** — for comment and newsletter collection
- **Disqus** — for public comment display
- **Netlify** — for website hosting
- **Pexels** — for licensed photography used in posts
